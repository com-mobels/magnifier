The app Magnifier is a modern electronic version of the well-known magnifying glass, that is usually used to view small objects.

If you need to view any small object or read small text, take out your phone and turn on the app Magnifier. Using slider at the bottom of the screen, you can zoom in and zoom out the image on you phone screen. When the magnification of the image is large, the image quality decreases and, therefore, sometimes it is better to turn down the magnification a little while improving the image quality. The app remember the selected magnification value and next time when you turn on the app again, the magnification value will be exactly the same as set before turning off thew app.

If the object you are looking at is poorly lit, turn on the backlight – to do this, press the button located at the bottom right of the screen/ Press this button again to turn off the backlight. The backlight turn off automatically when you exit the app.

The button located in the upper right part of the screen in intended for viewing Privacy Policy in accordance with that the app Magnifier was created and operated. The app does not request access to any confidential data that may be stored on your phone, and does not transfer any information regarding your personal data to third parties.

Unlike the usual traditional magnifier, the app Magnifier can also be used as electronic version of a monocular, with that you can zoom in objects located at a distance from you. The slider at the bottom of the screen work the same way as when looking at small objects.

The app is small – it take up just over 1.5 mb of memory. It is ready to right after installation, does not any configuration and work very quickly.

The developer hope that the app Magnifier will be a good help for you.
